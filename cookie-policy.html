1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device by websites you visit. They are stored by your web browser and contain information that can be read by the website on subsequent visits. Cookies are widely used across the internet to make websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide analytical information to site operators.

Cookies can be classified by several characteristics:

  • First-party cookies are set by the website you are currently visiting (in this case, rothiracalculator.app)
  • Third-party cookies are set by a domain other than the one you are visiting, typically through embedded content or services
  • Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period of time or until you manually delete them

2. How We Use Cookies

This website does not set any first-party cookies. We have made a deliberate design decision to avoid using cookies entirely. Our calculator processes all data client-side in your browser's memory without needing cookies for session management, user preferences, or any other purpose.

Specifically, we do not use cookies for:

  • Authentication or session management — there is no login system or user accounts
  • Personalization or preferences — calculator settings are not saved between visits
  • Analytics or tracking — we do not use cookie-based analytics platforms such as Google Analytics
  • Advertising — there are no ads on this website and no advertising cookies
  • Social media integration — there are no social media plugins, share buttons, or embedded social content that would set cookies

5. How to Manage Cookies

Although our website does not set cookies, third-party services may. You have full control over cookies through your browser settings. You can block, delete, or manage cookies at any time. Blocking all cookies will not affect the functionality of our calculator.

Instructions for managing cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome — Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox — Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari — Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge — Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies
  • Mobile browsers — check your browser's settings or preferences menu for cookie management options

You can also use browser extensions such as uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, or Cookie AutoDelete for more granular control over cookies and tracking.

6. Alternatives to Google Fonts

If you are concerned about Google Fonts requests, you have several options:

  • Use a content blocker — browser extensions like uBlock Origin can block requests to fonts.googleapis.com. The calculator will still function normally but will fall back to system fonts.
  • Use the Brave browser — Brave blocks third-party requests by default and proxies Google Fonts through its own servers.
  • Self-host — if you fork the open-source project, you can download the fonts and serve them from your own domain.

7. EU Cookie Compliance (ePrivacy Directive)

Under the EU ePrivacy Directive (often called the "Cookie Law") and GDPR, websites must obtain user consent before setting non-essential cookies. Since our website does not set any first-party cookies and does not use cookie-based analytics or advertising, we believe a cookie consent banner is not required for our site. The third-party fonts loaded from Google are considered a legitimate interest for delivering the Service's intended design.

If EU or other regulatory guidance changes to require consent for any technology we use, we will update our practices accordingly.
