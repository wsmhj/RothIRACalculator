2. Description of the Service

The Service is a free, open-source, browser-based financial calculator designed for educational and informational purposes. It provides the following tools:

  • Roth IRA Growth Projection — estimates your potential Roth IRA balance at retirement based on age, contributions, and expected returns
  • Eligibility Checker — determines your eligibility to contribute to a Roth IRA based on IRS income limits and filing status
  • Roth vs Traditional IRA Comparison — compares after-tax retirement values using a tax savings reinvestment model
  • Roth vs Taxable Account Comparison — compares Roth IRA returns with a taxable brokerage account, accounting for dividend taxes and capital gains
