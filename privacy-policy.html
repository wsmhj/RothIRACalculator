1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Roth IRA Calculator ("we," "us," or "our"), operated at rothiracalculator.app, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website ("Service"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

By using our Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). You are not required to provide your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, or any other personal identifier to use this Service. There is no user account system, no registration process, and no login functionality.

2.2 Financial Data

We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any financial data you enter into the calculator. All calculations — including income figures, tax rates, contribution amounts, and projected balances — are performed entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. This data exists only in your browser's volatile memory (RAM) and is never sent to our servers or any third-party server. When you close the browser tab or navigate away from the page, this data is permanently discarded.

2.3 Automatically Collected Technical Data

When you visit our website, our web hosting provider may automatically collect certain non-personally-identifiable technical data through standard server logs. This may include:

  • IP address — used for basic server operations and may be logged temporarily by the hosting provider
  • Browser type and version — such as Chrome 120, Firefox 121, Safari 17
  • Operating system — such as Windows, macOS, iOS, Android
  • Device type — desktop, tablet, or mobile
  • Referring URL — the website you visited immediately before ours
  • Pages visited and timestamps — which pages you accessed and when
  • Approximate geographic location — derived from IP address at the country or region level, not precise geolocation

This data is collected in aggregate form, is not linked to any personal identity, and is used solely for understanding general traffic patterns, monitoring server health, and improving the Service.

3. How We Use Information

The limited technical data we may collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service operation — ensuring the website loads correctly and performs reliably
  • Traffic analysis — understanding general usage patterns such as peak traffic times, popular pages, and geographic distribution of visitors
  • Security monitoring — detecting and preventing abuse, denial-of-service attacks, or other malicious activity
  • Service improvement — identifying technical issues, browser compatibility problems, or areas where the user experience can be enhanced

We do not use any collected information for advertising, marketing, user profiling, behavioral targeting, or sale to third parties.
